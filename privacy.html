1. Introduction

Welcome to FreshPlay Arcade ("we," "our," or "us"). We are an online arcade game web portal dedicated to providing users with browser-based gaming entertainment. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information you share with us.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and use our services. By accessing or using FreshPlay Arcade, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this Policy, please refrain from using our website.

2. Information We Collect

In order to provide and improve our gaming services, we may collect the following types of information:

A. Automatically Collected Information

When you access FreshPlay Arcade, we automatically collect certain technical information about your device and your visit. This does not directly reveal your specific identity but may include:

  • Device and Network Data: Your IP address, browser type and version, operating system, and device type.
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, the time and date of your visit, the time spent on those pages, referring/exit URLs, and clickstream data.
  • Gameplay Data: High scores, achievements, game progress, and interactions within supported browser-based games.

B. Information You Provide to Us

If we offer user accounts or you choose to contact us directly, we may collect:

  • Account Information: If you register for an account, we may collect your chosen username, email address, and password.
  • Communications: Any information you provide when you contact our support team, participate in surveys, or report a problem with our website.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for various operational and business purposes, including to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the FreshPlay Arcade web portal and its games.
  • Improve, personalize, and expand our website's layout, content, and gaming offerings.
  • Understand and analyze how you use our website to monitor trends and user behavior.
  • Develop new products, services, features, and functionality.
  • Serve relevant advertisements and manage our advertising network.
  • Communicate with you for customer service, updates, and website-related information.
  • Find and prevent fraud, spam, and security breaches to ensure compliance with our Terms of Service.

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

FreshPlay Arcade uses cookies, web beacons, and similar tracking technologies to enhance your user experience.

  • Cookies: Small data files stored on your device that help us remember your preferences, track user trends, and facilitate login sessions.
  • Managing Cookies: You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. However, if you choose to disable cookies, some portions or features of our games and website may not function properly.

5. Third-Party Services & Advertising

As a free-to-play arcade portal, we rely on third-party services to host games, analyze traffic, and display advertisements.

  • Third-Party Game Developers: Some of the browser-based HTML5 games on FreshPlay Arcade are provided by independent third-party developers. While playing these games, limited technical data may be processed by them to ensure proper game functionality.
  • Advertising Partners: We use third-party advertising companies (such as Google AdSense/Ad Exchange) to serve ads when you visit our website. These partners may use cookies to serve personalized ads based on your prior visits to our website and other websites on the Internet.
  • Analytics Providers: We use analytics tools (such as Google Analytics) to monitor and analyze web traffic and user behavior.

Note: We do not control the privacy practices of these third parties. You may opt out of certain personalized advertising by visiting the respective ad network's consumer opt-out pages (e.g., Google’s Ad Settings).

6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following situations:

  • With Service Providers: We may share data with trusted vendors, hosting providers, and technical partners who assist us in operating the website and delivering services to you, strictly under confidentiality agreements.
  • For Legal Reasons: We will disclose your information where required to do so by law, in response to a valid subpoena, court order, or governmental request.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your information may be transferred. We will provide notice before your personal information is transferred and becomes subject to a different Privacy Policy.

7. Data Security

We take the security of your information seriously and implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your data, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

8. Third-Party Links

FreshPlay Arcade may contain links to external websites, application stores, or third-party services that are not operated by us. If you click on a third-party link, you will be directed to that third party's site. We strongly advise you to review the Privacy Policy of every site you visit. We have no control over and assume no responsibility for the content, privacy policies, or practices of any third-party sites or services.

9. Children’s Privacy

FreshPlay Arcade is not intended for children under the age of 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under this age without verifiable parental consent. If you are a parent or guardian and you believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately so that we can take necessary actions to remove such data from our servers.

10. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction (such as the GDPR for European users or CCPA for California residents), you may have specific rights regarding your personal data, including:

  • The right to access the personal information we hold about you.
  • The right to request that we correct any inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • The right to request the deletion of your personal data.
  • The right to object to or restrict certain processing activities.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.

Any changes will be effective immediately upon posting the updated Policy on this page, with a revised "Effective Date" at the top. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your information.
